Cars in Switzerland

In These Municipalities People Like To Drive Sport Utility Vehicles

More and more SUVs are are sold in Switzerland. A new analysis now shows in which Swiss Regions these Cars are really popular.

By Dominik Balmer

Published July 26, 2019

Lens and Evolène in the Valais, Homberg in the Berner Oberland: In these three Swiss Municipalities Sport Utility Vehicles (SUV) are most popular.

This is the result of a new analysis of data from the Swiss Federal Road Office.

Surprisingly not the big Cities like Zürich, Geneva and Bern have the highest SUV quotas.

The Results on a Map

The analysis shows how many SUVs are licensed in each Municipality - for a better understanding the results are calculated per 1000 Inhabitants.
